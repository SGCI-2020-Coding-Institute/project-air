Stay Updated!

The COVID-19 pandemic is dangerous and unpredictable. We created this site to try and help keep users informed with the death rates of COVID-19 and other viruses to show users how the death rates compare.

Our Goal

The purpose of the data is to compare the COVID-19 mortality rate to other deadly viruses we've had in the past.

Help us help you...

We give you the option to upload your own csv file with data on cases and deaths of other viruses or tragedies so you can compare with the data provided.
