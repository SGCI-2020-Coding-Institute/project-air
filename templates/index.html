Stay Updated!

The Coronavirus pandemic that we are in is dangerous and unpredictable. However, we created this site to keep you informed with the data so you can stay prepared by examining the data we have shared with you. This will show you if the spread of the virus is speeding up or slowing down.

Our Goal

The purpose of the data is to compare the coronavirus mortality rate to other deadly viruses we've had in the past. In doing so, you will be informed if there are any precautions that are necessary to take and what you can look forward to in the future.

Help us help you...

We give you the option to upload your own data so you can compare with the data provided.
