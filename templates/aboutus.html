About our Project

We provide the death rate of COVID-19 and allow users to upload a csv file with cases and deaths of other known viruses to show the similarities or differences of the death rates. The comparisons will also reveal which direction the COVID-19 death rates are moving, from the comparisons the user will gain insight on the death rate of COVID compared to other viruses, pandemics, or epidemics which will in turn, give the user insight on preparing for the next steps.
